简介：
The Baiyun Temple in Beijing is a renowned Taoist temple. It embodies profound religious and cultural connotations. With its ancient architecture and serene atmosphere, it represents Taoist philosophy and pursuit. It is a place where people can experience the wisdom and tranquility of Taoism.
推荐书目：
1. "Taoist Buildings" : This book introduces many famous Taoist temples, including Baiyun Temple. It elaborates on the distribution, design, site selection, layout, structure and other aspects of Taoist architecture, so that readers can deeply understand the religious culture and artistic value contained in the architecture of Baiyun Temple.
2. "The Taoists of Peking, 1800-1949" : The book conducts an in-depth study of Daoism and Taoist priests in Beijing, with part of the book focusing on Taoist priests and related systems in Baiyun Temple, including the history of Baiyun Temple, abbot and key positions, Taoist residence system, economic conditions, and connections with other Taoist temples, etc., which will help readers to fully understand the religious culture and historical development of Baiyun Temple.
