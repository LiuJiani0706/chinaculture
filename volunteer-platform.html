CCIB北京大学生志愿者组织介绍：

CCIB College Student Volunteer Organisation is committed to helping foreign tourists better understand Beijing. Composed of college students from various universities in Beijing, the organisation provides free online consultation and offline tour guide services. Whether it is planning the itinerary, understanding the local culture, or accompanying the tour on the spot, the Beijing CCIB University Volunteer Organisation wholeheartedly provides all-round help and support for tourists.

Your donation will be used in multiple fields to ensure that the CCIB College Student Volunteer Organisation can provide continuous and high-quality services. Specific uses include:

1. Volunteer training and professional skills improvement: It includes courses on tour guide skills, cross-cultural communication ability, first aid knowledge, etc. Part of the donations will be used to hire professional lecturers, and subscribe to online courses.

2. Promotion activities: In order to let more foreign tourists know about our services, we will promote the organisation through a variety of channels, including maintaining social media platforms, shooting introduction videos, etc.

3. Support for daily operating expenses: Although we are a volunteer organisation, daily operations still need financial support. Donations will be used to cover necessary operating expenses, such as website maintenance, event venue leasing, communication and office equipment procurement. These expenses ensure we can maintain normal operations and provide visitors with a smooth service experience.

4. Organisation and planning of cultural exchange activities: We will regularly hold special cultural activities, such as Beijing traditional food experience, calligraphy and tea art experience, historical site tour. These activities not only enrich the travel experience of tourists, but also give volunteers the opportunity to gain an in-depth understanding of their own culture, share and communicate with tourists during the activities.

Thank you for your support, with which we can continue to help more foreign tourists discover the unique beauty of Beijing!
