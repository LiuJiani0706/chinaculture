简介：
The Confucius Temple in Beijing, built in 1302, is rich in cultural heritage. It houses ancient steles and inscriptions. The magnificent architecture reflects traditional wisdom. It's a place where one can feel the respect for knowledge and morality. A symbol of China's profound traditional culture, it offers a glimpse into the intellectual and moral pursuits of ancient times.
推荐书目：
1. "Confucianism and Sacred Space: The Confucius Temple from Imperial China to Today" : This book deeply discusses the sacred status of Confucian temples from ancient China to modern times and the significance of their space in Confucian culture, which is of great help to understanding the cultural heritage and historical changes of Beijing Confucian temples.
2. "The Pageant of Peking" : This is a collection of photographs by Donald Mennie, an American photographer, which includes such scenic spots as the Temple of Confucius in Beijing and the life scenes in the streets of Beijing at that time, so that you can experience the historical features of the temple of Confucius in Beijing from a visual perspective.
