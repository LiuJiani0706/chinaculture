简介：
The Lama Temple (Yonghegong) is a significant religious site. As a former palace and later a lamasery, it combines imperial grandeur and profound Buddhist culture. With its magnificent architecture and serene atmosphere, it represents the rich religious heritage and spiritual pursuit. It is a place of pilgrimage and cultural exploration.
推荐书目：
1. "Lama Temple (Yonghegong)", published by Foreign Languages Press, introduces the whole picture of the Lama Temple in English and Chinese, helping readers to have a comprehensive understanding of the history, architecture, religion and culture of the Lama Temple.
2. "Temples and Monasteries in Beijing" : Beijing Temples and Taoist Temples published by Foreign Languages Press introduce the temples and Taoist temples in Beijing in English, which will involve the relevant content of the Lama Temple, so that readers can understand the overall situation of religious places in Beijing and have a deep understanding of the status and characteristics of the Lama Temple in it.
