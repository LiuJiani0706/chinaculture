简介
The Forbidden City embodies a fusion of ideological and cultural elements. It represents traditional Chinese architecture and reflects the pursuit of harmony and order. It showcases imperial culture and contains profound philosophical concepts. It is a masterpiece that combines artistry and ideology.
推荐书目
1. The Forbidden City (Wonders of the World) by Geremie R. Barme is published by Harvard University Press. The book deeply discusses the cultural, political and architectural aspects of the Forbidden City, analyzes the Chinese history and cultural connotation of the Forbidden City, and presents readers with a comprehensive and unique image of the Forbidden City.
2. Beijing Forbidden City (English version) : This is one of the series of books on the popularization and inheritance of ancient Chinese architecture knowledge. The book is divided into three parts to introduce the planning, construction and inner court of the Forbidden City in detail, through the analysis and interpretation of architectural data, to help readers understand the design ideas and methods of ancient craftsmen, as well as the aesthetic taste and fun life of users such as the Emperor Queen.
3. Great Palace Museum Treasure (English version) : Compiled by Yujian Culture of the Palace Museum Publicity and Education Department, published by Tourism Education Press. In the form of children's picture books, through interesting stories and wonderful pictures, the unique charm and spiritual connotation of the 12 cultural relics of the Forbidden City are displayed to readers around the world, which helps children and teenagers understand the culture of the Forbidden City.
